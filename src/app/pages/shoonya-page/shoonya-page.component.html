OUR WAY: The cornerstones to all that we do

CURIOSITY & CREATIVITY

Students remain creative in their learning and expression, using imagination and insight to produce original and useful ideas and solutions.

APPLICATION OF EXPRESSION AND SELF-DIRECTEDNESS

Students are increasingly able to transfer curricular content and skills to new learning. They effectively represent and express their ideas in many genres and media and to varied audiences. Students are self -aware and reflective and are active participants in their own learning and development as people.

CULTURE OF TRUST

Students entrust themselves and others by understanding emotions and needs of their own and others. They demonstrate respect and empathy for others.

SUSTAINABLE WAY OF LIVING AND RESILIENCE

Students are prepared to make informed and take ethical decisions, taking responsibility for their own actions and becoming positive and helpful members of their communities. Students are always willing to be flexible, independent, and courageous in the pursuit of learning and persevere in the face of challenges.

LIFELONG LEARNING: ANYWHERE, EVERYWHERE AND ANYTIME

Students are prepared to meet the challenges of a demanding education and continue the path of lifelong learning. They develop skills in critical and logical thinking, using analysis and evaluation to come to reasoned judgments right from the beginning.
