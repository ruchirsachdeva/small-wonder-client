Curriculum

We take an inquiry based experiential learning approach to promote cognitive, social and emotional development and ensure our students have a solid foundation for lifelong learning. We ensure students master developmental milestones in a safe and conducive learning environment.

Drama and Theatre

Drama classes enhance creativity and confidence through role-playing, improvisation, and performances. Students improve communication, teamwork, and self-expression while enjoying the transformative experience of bringing characters and stories to life on stage

Sports

Our sports program develops discipline, teamwork, and leadership through football, basketball, cricket, volleyball, archery, shooting, swimming, badminton, and more. Expert coaches train students, fostering fitness, perseverance, and a passion for healthy competition.

Instrumental Music

Students learn instruments like keyboard, guitar, and drums, exploring rhythm, melody, and theory. This program nurtures patience, coordination, and musical creativity, helping students express themselves through instrumental art.

Dance and Yoga

Students explore traditional and modern dance styles, learning choreography while improving physical coordination, flexibility, and discipline. Dance fosters self-expression, creativity, and confidence in a joyful, artistic environment.

Indoor Games

Indoor games like chess, table tennis, and board games teach patience, strategy, and focus. Our play area offers a fun space for cognitive development, relaxation, and building social connections.

Robotics and AI

Students explore innovation by building and programming robots while learning AI principles. This hands-on program enhances critical thinking, problem-solving, and technological skills, preparing students to become future innovators and tech leaders.

Public Speaking

Public speaking programs teach students to articulate ideas confidently through debates, elocutions, and presentations. They build critical thinking, persuasive expression, and communication skills essential for leadership.

AR and VR

AR and VR programs immerse students in history, science, and geography, enabling them to explore distant planets, dive into human anatomy, or travel back in time, making learning unforgettable and engaging

Coding

Coding classes teach programming, algorithms, and logic through fun projects like games and apps. Students build essential digital skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities for future success in the technology-driven world.

Art and Craft

Art sessions inspire creativity as students work with mediums like paint, clay, and fabric. These activities enhance fine motor skills, aesthetic appreciation, and imaginative self-expression, fostering a love for art.
