Establishing a love for learning

Our youngest learners are cared for and taught crucial developmental skills by doing what they love best - playing. We take an experiential approach, so your child will be an active participant in their own learning. Through music, creative movement, and sensory activities we help your child hit developmental milestones and solidify the essentials including letters, numbers, shapes, and colors.

Focusing on the whole child

Children in grades KG - 2 thrive in an experiential environment designed to leverage their natural curiosity and joy of learning. Empowered problem solvers and collaborators are encouraged to think big, synthesize and test ideas and celebrate the learning process. Academics integrated with movement, fine arts, character- based leadership .

Challenging every child

Uncovering academic passions, moral compass setting, social skills and leadership development are the work of students in grades 3-5. Small wonders engages this dynamic window of development with academic personalization, mentoring relationships and experiential learning to develop students who are confident to embrace and share their gifts to impact the world.

Sustaining momentum

In grades 6 and over we develop children who are capable, self-reliant and have a strong sense of self. Our classroom environments are developmentally appropriate, well prepared, positive and warm. We welcome each child to engage in building skills of independence, concentration, order and coordination. Through hands-on, self directed, and collaborative learning, children learn a plethora of skills.
